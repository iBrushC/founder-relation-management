# Founder Relation Management ([sfrm.network](sfrm.network))
This is a simple open source platform for managing relations and projects, made by student founders for student founders. Helps with tracking connections, communications, events, projects, and more. This is the repository for people who wish to modify/host their own version of the project or understand how it works. If this doesn't interest you, you can use it for free (or $1 per week for extra features) on the [official website](sfrm.network).

## Key Features
As a student founder, the main problems I wanted to solve were that keeping up with people is not trivial on top of working and school. Additionally, I'm frequently involved in different projects involving different people and timelines, and keeping track of this between multiple apps grew annoying.

### People Tracking
Record people you've met, keep notes, track interactions, and get reminders to check up on people. Set dates associated with people for things like deliverable deadlines or birthdays.

### Project Tracking
Keep track of everything relating to a specific project or campaign. Companies, outreach, funding, and timeline all in one place
